About Me

I am a fullstack software developer and a technology enthusiast, whose parents never had an opportunity to attain school even for a single day in their entire life. I cannot forget days until spring of 2017 that only electronic devices in my house were 24" TV that would run local channels, and a simple cell phone.

Once I bought my first ever computer in Spring 2017, my curiosity as to how it was programmed deciphered too deep in me; the consequences of which triggered me to learn new technology each day. Even though I pursued my Bachelors' degree in Mathematics in 2019, my curiosity did not change, thus, derailing me from my original intention of attending college to be a high school Math teacher. I decided not to give up the full time survival job(Customer Service Representative/Manager at a Retail Market) that I was holding while attending undergraduate college full time. Dedicately spending 3-4 months of aftermath of graduating from college, in researching, how I should I be moving forward, I made a bold decision to pursue graduate studies in computer science while also attending intensive coding bootcamp on top of my fulltime survival job.

The first quarter of 2020 was spent on successfully accomplishing the GRE(Graduate Ready Exam) to apply for graduate studies in Computer Science. Then in Spring of 2021, I also joinned an intensive coding bootcamp to enhance my coding skills with an optimistic intention of accomplishing it by the end of 2022, which fortunately turned out to be a reality. I am also close to getting my MS in Computer Science. I still hold a fulltime survival job. The Coding Bootcamp provided me the pragmatic eperience and the job ready skills, while the Graduate Studies ehanced my theoretical and research skills.

Besides academia and work: I program and write code 30 minutes-2 hours each day. I love to play cultural musical instruments(such as Madal), and the cricket(sports). I am an active member of the cricket club in Aurora, Colorado. This is my second tennure as the President of the Aurora Everest Cricket Club.
